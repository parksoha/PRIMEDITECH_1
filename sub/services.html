Turning

High-efficiency cutting machining for precision and mass production

PRIMEDITECH provides high-precision turning services using the latest CNC lathe equipment to precisely machine rotating materials with cutting tools. With specialized technical capabilities for cylindrical and shaft component manufacturing, we machine various metal components including shafts, nozzles, and fittings to within ±0.005mm precision.

Through thermal deformation compensation technology of the latest equipment and automatic tool change systems (16+ Tool Stations), we achieve high-speed, high-precision, and high-efficiency production, flexibly responding from small-batch multi-variety to mass production.

We provide turning solutions optimized for industrial sectors requiring high quality and repeatability, including medical devices, aerospace, semiconductors, and precision machinery.

Turn-Mill Complex

Simultaneous turning + milling processes in a single equipment

PRIMEDITECH provides CNC complex machining services that simultaneously perform turning (rotary cutting) and milling (planar cutting) in a single equipment. This process enables continuous machining of various shaped components with a single clamping (fixing), simultaneously achieving process reduction, error minimization, and precision improvement.

Complex machining equipment can simultaneously drive rotating and fixed tools, enabling multi-surface machining of cylindrical, planar, and curved components. PRIMEDITECH efficiently produces complex shaped shafts, fittings, robot joints, and aerospace fittings through high-speed automatic tool change systems and CAD/CAM integrated control.

PRIMEDITECH possesses 5-axis CNC machining technology that simultaneously controls X, Y, Z axes + 2 rotary axes (A, B). This process enables machining complex 3D shapes from multiple directions with a single setup, simultaneously achieving process time reduction and precision improvement.

Utilizing the latest 5-axis equipment and advanced CAM software, we achieve ±0.001mm level precision, specializing in manufacturing high-difficulty curved and multi-surface components such as human body curved implants, aerospace engine parts, and semiconductor equipment covers.

Annealing

Heat treatment process that stabilizes metal microstructure

Annealing is a heat treatment process that heats metal to an appropriate temperature and then slowly cools it to remove internal stress and stabilize the microstructure. This process improves metal ductility and machinability while softening hardened structures.

PRIMEDITECH controls optimal annealing temperature and cooling rate for various metal materials to achieve uniform microstructure and excellent mechanical properties. It is mainly used for material softening before machining, internal stress removal, and microstructure homogenization.

Normalizing

Heat treatment for uniform ferrite-pearlite structure formation

Normalizing is a heat treatment process that heats steel above Ac3 temperature and then cools it in air to form a uniform ferrite-pearlite structure. This process improves the mechanical properties of steel and removes internal stress.

PRIMEDITECH ensures consistent normalizing quality through precise temperature control and cooling conditions. It is mainly used for cast product microstructure improvement, pre-machining treatment, and mechanical property enhancement.

QT (Quenching & Tempering)

Heat treatment process combining quenching and tempering

QT is a heat treatment process that combines quenching and tempering, significantly improving steel hardness and strength while ensuring appropriate toughness.

PRIMEDITECH performs optimal QT processes through precise temperature control and cooling medium selection. After forming martensite structure through quenching, we achieve a balance between hardness and toughness by relieving brittleness and improving toughness through tempering.

Vacuum Heat Treatment

High-quality heat treatment process performed in vacuum environment

Vacuum heat treatment is a heat treatment process performed in a vacuum environment, preventing oxidation or decarburization and improving surface quality. Heating and cooling in vacuum state minimizes surface contamination of components.

PRIMEDITECH performs high-quality vacuum heat treatment using vacuum furnaces with precise temperature control and atmosphere regulation. It is mainly used for high-quality heat treatment of aerospace, medical device, and precision machinery components.

Nitriding Heat Treatment

Surface hardening heat treatment through nitrogen penetration

Nitriding heat treatment is a surface hardening heat treatment process that penetrates nitrogen into metal surfaces to form high-hardness nitriding layers. This process significantly improves surface hardness and wear resistance while also improving corrosion resistance.

PRIMEDITECH applies various nitriding heat treatment methods including gas nitriding and plasma nitriding to form nitriding layers that meet component requirements. It is mainly used for improving wear resistance of gears, bearings, and shaft components.

Carburizing Heat Treatment

Surface hardening heat treatment through carbon penetration

Carburizing heat treatment is a surface hardening heat treatment process that penetrates carbon into metal surfaces to form high-hardness surface layers. This process significantly improves surface hardness and wear resistance while maintaining core ductility.

PRIMEDITECH applies various carburizing heat treatment methods including gas carburizing and liquid carburizing to form carburizing layers that meet component requirements. It is mainly used for improving wear resistance of gears, bearings, and shaft components.

High Frequency Heat Treatment

Selective heat treatment through high-frequency induction heating

High-frequency heat treatment is a heat treatment process that selectively heats only specific areas of components using high-frequency current induction heating, then rapidly cools to harden them. This process enables precise area hardening while minimizing deformation.

PRIMEDITECH selectively hardens only the required areas through high-frequency generation equipment and precise coil design. It is mainly used for local hardening of shaft components and surface hardening of gear components.

Surface Treatment

Electropolishing

Electrochemical surface polishing process

Electropolishing is a surface treatment technology that electrochemically dissolves metal surfaces to minimize surface roughness and maximize cleanliness and corrosion resistance. It uniformly removes micro-protrusions and machining marks that cannot be removed by conventional mechanical polishing, achieving surface roughness of Ra 0.2 μm or less and excellent gloss and corrosion resistance.

PRIMEDITECH achieves clean and uniform surface quality by precisely controlling current density, electrolyte composition, temperature, and dissolution rate for high-cleanliness alloy materials such as SUS303F, SUS316L, Inconel, and titanium.

It is mainly applied to industrial sectors requiring high cleanliness and corrosion resistance, including medical devices, semiconductors, vacuum equipment, and laboratory equipment.

샌드블라스팅 (Sand Blasting)

High-pressure micro-particle blasting surface treatment

This process blasts high-pressure micro-particles onto metal surfaces to remove impurities, oxide layers, and rough machined surfaces, uniformly processing the surface. It is used for surface cleanliness improvement, adhesion enhancement, and matte texture implementation, and is also essential as pretreatment for painting, plating, and anodizing.

PRIMEDITECH precisely controls particle size (50~200 μm) and blasting pressure (3~7 bar) to achieve textures and roughness suitable for product applications. We also use various blasting media including alumina, glass beads, ceramic, and steel shot to provide customized surface effects such as glossy, matte, and cleaning types.

It is mainly applied to pretreatment and uniform texture implementation of medical device housings, aircraft components, semiconductor equipment, and precision machinery frames.

아노다이징 (Anodizing)

Oxide film formation surface treatment

Anodizing is a process that forms artificial oxide films on metal surfaces to improve corrosion resistance and wear resistance. It is mainly applied to aluminum and aluminum alloy components, and can implement various colors, textures, and gloss effects while protecting the surface.

PRIMEDITECH precisely controls current, electrolyte concentration, time, and temperature to form uniform coating thickness (5~25 μm), and provides customized processes according to applications such as hard anodizing and color anodizing.

It is mainly used for corrosion resistance improvement and appearance quality enhancement of medical device housings and aerospace/robotics aluminum components.

Passivation

Chemical oxide film formation anti-corrosion process

Passivation is a chemical treatment process that removes impurities and free iron from stainless steel and alloy surfaces, forming protective oxide films based on Cr₂O₃. This process strengthens corrosion resistance and minimizes surface contamination.

PRIMEDITECH operates eco-friendly passivation processes using nitric and citric acid solutions, applying them to components requiring high cleanliness and corrosion resistance such as medical devices, semiconductors, and precision machinery parts.
